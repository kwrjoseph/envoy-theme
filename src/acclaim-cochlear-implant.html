No Externals.
Just Hearing.

The Acclaim® Cochlear Implant is designed to be the first-of-its-kind fully implanted cochlear implant. Unlike traditional cochlear implants, it requires no external audio processor, microphone, or magnet.

Natural Sound Capture

Designed to use the ear's natural anatomy to capture sound, leveraging the pinna and ear canal just like natural hearing.

24/7 Hearing

Hear around the clock. Sleep, shower, and swim without removing any device. Designed for true continuous hearing functionality.

Invisible Hearing®

Nothing to wear on the ear. Nothing to lose. A fully discreet solution that restores confidence along with hearing.

Technology

Innovative
Sensor Technology

The groundbreaking innovation behind the Acclaim is our proprietary **Sensor Technology**.

Instead of an external microphone, the Acclaim uses a sensor implanted in the middle ear to detect vibrations from the incus (anvil) bone. This allows the device to leverage the outer ear's natural sound-collecting capabilities.

  • 01

    Sound Enters Naturally

    Sound waves travel through the ear canal and vibrate the eardrum and hearing bones.

  • 02

    Sensor Detection

    The implanted sensor detects these mechanical vibrations and converts them into electrical signals.

  • 03

    Cochlear Stimulation

    The processor sends impulses to the electrode array in the cochlea, stimulating the hearing nerve.

"Leveraging the ear's natural anatomy for a better hearing experience."
