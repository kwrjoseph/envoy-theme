Featured Solution

Middle Ear Implants (MEI)

Directly stimulate the middle ear structures. These have a long history of safety and effectiveness but remain a relatively unknown option for many patients.

Learn about Middle Ear Implants
Featured Solution

Cochlear Implants (CI)

Bypass damaged portions of the inner ear and directly stimulate the auditory nerve using electrical signals. Ideal for severe to profound sensorineural loss.

Learn about Cochlear Implants

Other Considerations: Bone Conduction

Bone conduction implants transmit sound vibrations directly to the inner ear via the skull. Typical for conductive loss, mixed loss, or single-sided deafness. Please note: This option is not currently offered by Envoy Medical.
