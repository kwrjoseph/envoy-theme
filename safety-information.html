Contraindications

Medical Warning: The Esteem® System is not suitable for everyone. The following conditions are contraindicated for this device.

Ear Health History

  • History of post-adolescent chronic middle ear infections.
  • Inner ear disorders (e.g., Meniere's, Hydrops).
  • Recurring vertigo requiring treatment.
  • History of cholesteatoma or destructive middle ear disease.
  • Retrocochlear or central auditory disorders.

General Health & Anatomy

  • Pregnancy.
  • History of keloid formation.
  • Hypersensitivity to silicone, polyurethane, stainless steel, gold, or titanium.
  • Pre-existing conditions affecting healing.
  • Disabling tinnitus requiring treatment.

General Safety Information

Device Care & Handling

Patients should avoid pushing or twisting the implanted parts (Sensor and Driver leads) located under the skin. Excessive manipulation can lead to skin erosion or damage to the Esteem system components, potentially requiring surgical correction.

Travel & Security

  • Airport Security: The Esteem may activate metal detectors. Carry your Envoy Medical identification card at all times to show security personnel.
  • Interference: Metal detectors may temporarily interrupt hearing. Moving away from the source should restore normal function.
  • Pressure Changes: You may experience temporary "plugged" sensations during flight, similar to typical middle ear pressure changes.

Medical Procedures

Important: The Esteem device should always be turned OFF during medical therapies to prevent interference noises or damage.
  • Electrocautery: Must be bipolar only and never used directly over the implant. Can cause damage to the device or hearing.
  • Defibrillation: Turn device OFF during emergency use.
  • Radiation/X-Ray: X-Ray image quality near the implant may be compromised.

MRI Conditional Safety

MR CONDITIONAL

Safe for MRI under specific conditions

The implanted components of the Esteem® System are MR Conditional. A patient with this device can be safely scanned in an MR system meeting specific conditions.

1.5 Tesla Systems

  • Max field gradient: 1900 G/cm (19.0 T/m)
  • Sound processor can be present

3.0 Tesla Systems

  • Max field gradient: 1100 G/cm (with processor)
  • Max field gradient: 1900 G/cm (processor removed)

MRI Warnings

The Personal Programmer and other external accessories are MR Unsafe and must rarely enter the MRI room. Exposure to MRI scanning may reset the Sound Processor to factory settings.
