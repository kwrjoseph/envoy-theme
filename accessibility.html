Website Accessibility
Statement

Envoy Medical is committed to identifying solutions that make its website and partner websites available to as many people as possible and by meeting the requirements of the Americans with Disabilities Act. We strive to ensure that our website-based communications are accessible to those with visual, hearing, cognitive and motor impairments.

Our Commitment

Envoy Medical’s current website is ADA Compliant. We have recently reviewed our entire website to identify and correct any non-compliant elements.

We recognize that internet users with disabilities can find websites difficult to use. We are making every effort to ensure that our website is accessible to everyone. We are working to ensure that our website is accessible to potential customers with disabilities by using industry standard techniques and practices to provide an acceptable level of ADA accessibility for website users.
