The Evolution of
Hearing Technology

For decades, cochlear implants have relied on external hardware—microphones, processors, and batteries worn on the ear or head. While effective, these external components can limit life's most precious moments.

01

Traditional Era

External gear required. Removed for sleep, showers, and many physical activities.

The Fully Implanted Era

The Acclaim® CI is designed to be completely internal, using your ear's natural architecture as part of the system.

Invisible Innovation

No external hardware means you can finally forget about your hearing device and just listen to life.

The Science of
Internal Sensing

Most cochlear implants use external microphones. Envoy Medical's breakthrough technology uses an implanted **piezoelectric sensor** that captures sound exactly where nature intended.

Piezoelectric Tech

The sensor translates mechanical vibrations from the ear bone into electrical signals, similar to how a record player's needle works.

Natural Anatomy

By using the ear itself, the system preserves the natural directionality and filtering effects of your own ear shape.

Deeply Integrated

An internal battery provides power for daily use, recharged wirelessly while you relax or sleep.

Life Without
External Gear

Experience moments that were previously complicated or impossible with traditional processors.

Water Freedom

Shower, swim, and enjoy water activities without ever removing your hearing. True water resistance because it's under the skin.

Seamless Sleep

Hear the smoke alarm, your family, or the morning birds. No more silence as soon as the external gear comes off for bed.

Active Lifestyle

Helmets, glasses, and strenuous sweat no longer interfere with your hearing or the stability of a processor.
