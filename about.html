Who We Are

About Envoy Medical

We are dedicated to developing innovative hearing solutions that empower individuals to reclaim their hearing health, independence, and sense of adventure.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals to reclaim their hearing health, independence, and sense of adventure through innovative technologies. We aim to assist patients, friends, family, and health professionals in navigating the full spectrum of hearing loss treatment options, striving to identify and provide best-in-class hearing health solutions.

Our Vision

Our vision originated from a desire to create life-changing hearing technologies for those in need. We understand that each person has a unique motivation for living a meaningful life. Our commitment lies in developing innovative hearing solutions that enable people to live fully and actively while addressing their hearing loss.

Our History

1995

Company Founded

Envoy Medical Corporation was established to focus on innovative technologies across the hearing health spectrum.

2010

Esteem® Launch

The Esteem® active middle ear implant became commercially available in the U.S.

2019

FDA Recognition

The Acclaim® cochlear implant received Breakthrough Device Designation from the FDA.

2023

Public Company

Envoy Medical began trading on the Nasdaq Capital Market (Ticker: COCH) on October 2, 2023.
