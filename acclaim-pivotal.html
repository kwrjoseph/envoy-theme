Fully Implanted.
Naturally Designed.

The Acclaim® Cochlear Implant is an investigational device designed to be the first-of-its-kind fully implanted cochlear implant. Unlike traditional cochlear implants that rely on external microphones and processors, the Acclaim® CI is designed to leverage the unique anatomy of the ear.

It uses an implanted sensor to capture sound vibrations directly from the ear structure, eliminating the need for any external components on the head or ear during daily use.

Internal Sensor

Implanted piezoelectric sensor captures natural sound vibrations.

No External Gear

Designed to be invisible and unencumbered for 24/7 hearing.

The Pivotal Study

We are currently enrolling participants to evaluate the safety and performance of the Acclaim CI.

Eligibility Criteria

To participate in this study, candidates must meet specific medical and audiological requirements. Key criteria include:

  • 18 years of age or older
  • Diagnosis of severe to profound sensorineural hearing loss
  • Bilateral (both ears) hearing loss
  • Limited benefit from appropriately fitted hearing aids
  • English speaking and able to complete testing
  • Willingness to comply with study protocol

Note: Additional inclusion and exclusion criteria apply. A thorough evaluation by a study physician and audiologist is required to determine final eligibility.
