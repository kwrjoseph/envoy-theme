The groundbreaking innovation behind the Acclaim is our proprietary Sensor Technology.

Instead of an external microphone, the Acclaim uses a sensor implanted in the middle ear to detect vibrations from the incus (anvil) bone. This allows the device to leverage the outer ear’s natural sound-collecting capabilities.

01

Sound Enters Naturally

Sound waves travel through the ear canal and vibrate the eardrum and hearing bones.

02

Sensor Detection

The implanted sensor detects these mechanical vibrations and converts them into electrical signals.

03

Cochlear Stimulation

The processor sends impulses to the electrode array in the cochlea, stimulating the hearing nerve.
