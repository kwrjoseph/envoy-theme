The Esteem fully implanted active middle ear implant* is designed for adults with moderate to severe sensorineural hearing loss who want a discreet, maintenance-free alternative to traditional hearing aids.

You may be a candidate if you:

  • Are 18 years or older
  • Have stable, moderate to severe sensorineural hearing loss
  • Have healthy middle ear anatomy
  • Are seeking a fully implanted, 24/7 hearing solution
  • Prefer not to wear external hearing devices

Here are answers to some of the most common questions about the Esteem fully implanted active middle ear implant* and the Invisible Hearing experience:

What should I expect from the procedure?

The Esteem implant is placed entirely inside the body. It is performed under general anesthesia, typically takes 2–3 hours, and most patients return home the same day.

How long does the Esteem implant last?

The implant is built for long-term durability, with benchtop testing confirming a lifespan of at least 30 years. Many patients have had their implants for over 15 years.

Does the Esteem implant have a battery?

Yes. The battery and software are housed within the Sound Processor. Average battery life is approximately 5 years and replacement is a simple outpatient procedure (about 1 hour).

Battery life may be reduced to 2.8 years if continually exposed to very loud sound levels using the highest gain settings.

What if improvements are made to the implant?

The Esteem system is designed with backward compatibility. This means software or battery upgrades may be available during routine component replacements without a full system overhaul.
