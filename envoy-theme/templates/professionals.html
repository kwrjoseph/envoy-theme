The Esteem is designed for adults with moderate to severe sensorineural hearing loss. Use our clinical criteria to identify potential candidates in your clinic.

  • 18 years of age or older
  • Stable bilateral sensorineural hearing loss
  • Moderate to severe SNHL (defined by PTA)
  • Unaided SDS Score ≥ 40%
  • Normally functioning Eustachian tube
  • Normal middle ear anatomy & membrane
  • Adequate space (determined via CT scan)
  • Min. 30 days hearing aid experience
