The Esteem FI-AMEI leverages Nature’s Microphone technology to work with the ear’s natural anatomy, preserving sound integrity and minimizing distortion.

Three Implanted Components:

  • Sensor: Positioned on the incus bone, converts eardrum vibrations into electrical signals.
  • Sound Processor: Embedded in the temporal bone, customizes signals based on the patient’s hearing profile.
  • Driver: Translates signals into amplified vibrations that stimulate the cochlea.

The Esteem implant is indicated for patients who meet the following criteria:

  • 18 years and older (adults)
  • Stable, bilateral sensorineural hearing loss
  • Moderate to severe sensorineural hearing loss as defined by PTA (0.5, 1, 2 kHz)
  • Unaided speech discrimination (WRS) scores > 40%
  • No post-adolescent middle ear dysfunction
  • No conductive or mixed hearing losses
  • No history of major ear surgery
  • Minimum 30 days of appropriately fit hearing aid amplification device(s)
