Information We Collect

Envoy Medical collects two types of information from visitors to our website:

1. Personally Identifiable Information

This includes information that identifies you personally, such as your name, date of birth, telephone number, mailing address, email address, medical device serial number, or personal health information.

Important: We only collect this information when you voluntarily provide it to us, for example, by filling out a “Contact Us” form, submitting an audiogram, or sending us an email.

2. Access Information

This is information that does not identify you personally. It includes data like your IP address (in some contexts), browser type, and page visit history. We pool this data to create aggregate reports on website usage to improve our services.

Cookies

Envoy Medical uses “cookies” to enhance your experience. A cookie is a small text file placed on your computer’s browser. Cookies help us analyze web traffic and allow web applications to respond to you as an individual.

Your Choice: The information collected by cookies is not personally identifiable by itself. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings, though this may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.
