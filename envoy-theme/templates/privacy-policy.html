Envoy Medical Corporation (“Envoy Medical”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to envoymedical.com and outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to safeguard your personal data.

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time without notice. Your use of the Site signifies your assent to the policy at the time of your visit.

The Information We Collect

1. Access Information

This is information that does not identify you personally. It includes data like your device type, browser type, location, IP address, referral source, search terms used, pages viewed, links clicked, and time spent on pages. We pool this data to create aggregate reports on website usage to improve our services.

2. Personally Identifiable Information

This includes information that identifies you personally, such as your name, date of birth, telephone number, mailing address, email address, medical device serial number, or personal health information.

Important: We only collect this information when you voluntarily provide it to us, for example, by filling out a “Contact Us” form, submitting an audiogram, or sending us an email.

Cookies

Envoy Medical uses “cookies” to enhance your experience. A cookie is a small text file placed on your computer’s browser. Cookies help us store and track information to analyze web traffic and allow web applications to respond to you as an individual.

Your Choice: The information collected by cookies is not personally identifiable by itself. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings, though this may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to understand your needs and provide you with better service, specifically for the following reasons:

  • Internal record keeping.
  • Improving our products and services.
  • Responding to your inquiries and requests.
  • Maintaining records of our interactions with you.
  • Contacting you regarding products, clinical trials, or safety information (only if you have opted in).
  • Guiding research and development efforts.
  • Marketing and sales purposes (with your consent).
  • Compliance with legal requirements.

De-identified information (with all personal identifiers removed) may be used without restriction.

Disclosure to Third Parties

Envoy Medical will not share your Personally Identifiable Information with any unrelated third party without your permission, except as described below:

  • Healthcare Providers: We may share information with your physician or healthcare provider upon your request or if necessary for your safety.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with companies that perform services on our behalf in the ordinary course of business. They are strictly prohibited from using your information for other purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.

Children’s Privacy

The Envoy Medical website is not targeted to children under the age of 18 and we do not intentionally collect personal information from such children. If we discover that personal information from a child under 18 has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at legal@envoymedical.com.

Envoy Medical will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.
